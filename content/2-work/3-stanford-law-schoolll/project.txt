Text: When institutions recount their history, they typically highlight landmark dates and standard-setting achievements. “60+ Moments in the History of Stanford Law School,” an exhibit in Stanford Law School's Classroom Building, takes a less conventional approach, sharing the story of Stanford Law through a sampling of fact, anecdote, and recollection—famous, exploring Stanford Law’s origins, turning points, impact, innovations, history-makers, heroes, lore, and legend.

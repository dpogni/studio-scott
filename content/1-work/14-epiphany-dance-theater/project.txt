Text: Epiphany Dance Theater is a San Francisco-based contemporary performance company focused on large-scale interdisciplinary collaborations that blend dance, music, theater and spoken word into multi-textured experiences of community, culture, and place. Founded and originally branded in 1997, the artistic director Kim Epifano wanted a new brand identity and website to celebrate its 20th year that expressed the movement, energy, community, and wabi-sabi nature of their work.

Text: Heritage Foods is a dedicated network of sustainable, multi-generational family farmers who share a passion for raising hogs and producing farm-to-table Heirloom pork.

----

Readmoretext: The identity design was inspired by wrought iron signage prevalent on North American farms. An adult and juvenile Berkshire hog are nestled together, reinforcing family farming as well as the humane treatment and care of the animals.

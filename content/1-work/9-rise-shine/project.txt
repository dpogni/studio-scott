Coblentz Patch Duffy & Bass and the American Association of Advertising Agencies / 4A’s hosted their 9th annual morning program on October 18, 2018, featuring speakers to look at marketing from various angles—from M&A, technology, and agency structure, 
to the marketing of performing arts and artists. We were pleased to be asked to design their invitation and posters this year, joining a list of past designers that include Kit Hinrichs, Rob Duncan, and Primo Angeli.
